Reyzak is a casino game that utilizes two spinners, producing random numbers
from the ranges 2 to 5 and 0 to 7. 
Each player starts with 50 zephy of the Rigel-7 Zephy currency
The goal of Reyzak is to get as close as possible to 17 without going over.
After placing a bet, players must choose one of the spinners.
A player can spin as many times as they'd like before reaching 17.
If a player opts not to spin or reaches 17 the game pays out according 
to the chart listed below

>17	 0%
